Short Bio

I am a second year master student at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before that, I did my undergraduate at University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Yiqiang Chen. Previously, I received the B. E. degree from Chongqing University in 2020.

My research interest lies in machine learning, federated learning, Knowledge Distillation, Incremental Learning. Recently I focus on the Non_IID problem in federated learning.
